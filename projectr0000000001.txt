# 💰 Money Guide AI

An AI-powered financial planning application designed to help freelancers, entrepreneurs, and individuals manage their finances, track goals, and receive personalized financial guidance.

- 🤖 AI-powered financial assistant
- 📊 Smart expense and income tracking
- 🎯 Financial goal planning
- 📈 Budget recommendations
- 💡 Personalized financial insights
- 📅 Monthly spending analysis
- 🔒 Secure and user-friendly interface

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. Open the Live Demo.
2. Create your financial profile.
3. Add your income and expenses.
4. Set your financial goals.
5. Receive AI-powered recommendations.
6. Monitor your financial progress over time.
